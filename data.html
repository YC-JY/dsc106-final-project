The Development of a Legend Game Franchise

Growing in Popularity Over the Years

Sales of Animal Crossing: New Horizons in the first 6 weeks eclipsed the lifetime sales of every previous Animal Crossing title.

Sales Driver for Nintendo Switch

The launch of Animal Crossing: New Horizons has seen a huge spike in Nintendo Switch console sales. The sales of NS is about 400% up over the previous week.

Popular in Every State!

Animal Crossing: New Horizons is popular in the United States, especially in western states, and has 100 search interest in Hawaii.

Both Male and Female are Attracted by Animal Crossing!

Normally there are more male than female console players (70% vs. 30%). However, according to the investigation on Animal Crossing: New Horizons players, the ratio of men and women is balanced (48.7% vs. 51.3%).

Nintendo has many female developers in its software development departments.

Kyogoku, the current manager of Nintendo's Entertainment Planning & Development (EPD) Group No. 5, is the producer, director, and supervisor of Animal Crossing series since 2008.
